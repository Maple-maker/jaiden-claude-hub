Step 01

The blind-advice problem

Ask any model about rebalancing, debt, or risk and it answers from population averages — it has no idea what you hold, owe, or earn. Confident, generic, and not actually about you.

The gapAdvice that doesn't know your numbers is entertainment, not strategy.
Step 02

What Era Context is

A connector that holds your live financial picture — positions, balances, debts — and exposes it to Claude on demand. Nothing is hardcoded or snapshotted; it's one source of truth read in real time.

Your finances aren't a static snapshot. They're a live position that changes daily.

PrincipleLive data on demand beats a snapshot that's stale the moment you save it.
Step 03

How it works

Before answering anything about money, Claude queries Era Context, loads the current numbers, and reasons over them. It never advises from memory or a month-old figure.

Garbage context in, garbage advice out — so the context has to be live.

The ruleCheck the live ledger first. Then advise.
Step 04

How I use it to sharpen my finances

The debt-paydown cascade is ranked against real balances. BTC conviction and an income layer are sized to actual cash flow. Roth and core compounding get a weekly review on real data — not vibes.

This is the Financially-Free lethality in practice: a money system you can query, not a feeling you chase.

GuardrailKeep real figures out of public content. The system is shareable; the balances are not.
